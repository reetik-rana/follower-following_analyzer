Instagram Follower Analyzer

Important points to know before using:
  • This app is designed to run locally on your computer. You must install Python, Chrome/Chromium/Brave, and ChromeDriver as described in the README.
  • You must be logged in to your Instagram account in the browser profile used by the app for it to access your followers and following lists.
  • If you are not logged in, the app will not be able to access your followers or following lists.
  • The app works for your own account (private or public), public accounts, and private accounts you follow.
  • It cannot access private accounts you do not follow.
  • Your Instagram session is used for scraping; no credentials are stored by the app.
  • Instagram may block or limit automation. Use responsibly and avoid frequent requests.
  • If you encounter errors, check your browser and ChromeDriver installation, and make sure the paths are set correctly in webapp/app.py.
